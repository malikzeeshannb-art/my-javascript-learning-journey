5. Remainder (%)
What I learned:
The % operator tells what remains after division.
My understanding (in my own words):
“The % operator gives the leftover value after division.”
This was the final checkpoint — and my explanation was correct.
Example I wrote (with a correction):
❌ My mistake:
I wrote console.loge instead of console.log.
✔️ Corrected code:
let totalDays = 407;
let oneBlock = 10;

let remainingBlock = totalDays % oneBlock;
console.log(remainingBlock); // 7
6. My Confusion & How I Solved It
Confusion:
I didn’t understand how 365 % 7 becomes 1.
Solution (explained and understood):
7 × 52 = 364
365 − 364 = 1
So 365 ÷ 7 = 52 weeks + 1 leftover day
That leftover day is the remainder.
Once explained in Roman Urdu, the concept became fully clear for me.
7. Final Understanding of %
% = divide, aur jo bacha hua hissa ho, woh return karta hai
Or in English:
“% returns the remainder after division.”
